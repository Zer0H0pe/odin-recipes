Shells with parmesan cheese

So for this recipe you basically boil water, pour in half a box of medium shells and cook until desired tenderness.

Then you drain and dump in a bowl. Cover with parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Medium shells
  • Water
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Pour in pasta
  3. Cook 7 minutes
  4. Drain
  5. Add parmesan
